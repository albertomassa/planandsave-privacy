Privacy Policy – Plan & Save

Last updated: March 2026

No Data Collected

Plan & Save does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. All data you enter in the app (income, expenses, categories, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

No Third-Party Services

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or third-party SDKs. There are no trackers of any kind.

No Account Required

Plan & Save works completely offline. No account, no registration, no cloud sync is required or available.

Children's Privacy

Since Plan & Save does not collect any data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.
